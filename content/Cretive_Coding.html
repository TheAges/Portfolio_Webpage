Don’t wake up Santa!

Christmas hackathon
Dicember 2017

Sketch made in occasion of a Christmas theme codejam. It presents in a playful and satirical way how Christmas is presented and celebrated in other sites and portals. The code uses the device's microphone to detect the noise level and change Santa Claus status according to the sourround noise.

SPACE CLOCK

3D Clock
Ottobre 2017

Clock placed "in depth" that indicates both the current time (from left to right), the day of the month (bottom) and the month (top).

Triangular Texture

Scale > Rotate > Invert > Repeat
Settembre 2017

Self-generated, interactive pattern inspired by Franco Grignani's Op art. it is based on a very simple algorithm that draws a triangle and scaling it, rotating it, inverting the colors.
