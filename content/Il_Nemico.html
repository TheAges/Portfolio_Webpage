The Enemy

From illustrated book to Multimedia product.
Book App from picture book - June 2016

Multimedia Translation, from picture books to book app of "the Enemy", by Davide Calì and Serge Bloch. It consists of a transposition into a multimedia application of the history and contents of the book. For this application we started from the deconstruction of the main stylistic and narrative elements, to then recontextualize them in a new interactive digital environment. A video mock-up was then produced to show the interactions in the key moments to the story.

Picture book analysis

We started from the original material, the paper-based picture book. From here we disassembled the story elements and the narrative structure in many sub “scenes”, reassembled together in a linear style, like in the original book.
Storyboard

Then we thought all the interaction that the user had to do in order to complete the story and interact with the book, then we produced a storyboard in order to arrange all the elements that we had to produce and animate, as well all the on screen interactions with the user.

Mock-up book app

Finally, we produced all the animation and multimedia elements that would help tell and enrich the story narrate with a digital device and not anymore with a physical support. Then we showed all the work with a short video mock-up, showing the most meaningful moment of the application.
